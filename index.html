An Argument by Demonstration

This site works as an argument against the claim made by this essay.

Here's the basic idea of the argument. The essay by Alberto Romero says that visual artists are really worried about AI because art is characterized by very distinctive stylistic elements -- and AI is really good at copying style. But style is much less important for writing, and what is important for writing is something AI just can't do so well.

This site tests the idea that style isn't such a big deal for writing. The method is simple. At a pretty frequent pace, links will be added below that showcase a pretty well-known passage or quote being rewritten by AI using a different rhetorical form -- a different style. You be the judge of how important the shift in style is.
